Privacy Policy

As a user of Chordwriter, you have the right to expect us to keep your information private. Our promise to you is that we will never sell or give access to your information. We will continue to use best practice techniques to keep your data secure.

What data do we collect?

We collect anonymized data about your usage of Chordwriter, including but not limited to, the types of files you open and edit, the features of the application you use. This data is collected without personal information, and supplied to the Store analytics.

How do we use your data?

We use the analytics data to continually improve the app, and determine which features are more or less useful for our users.

What data do we sell?

Absolutely nothing.

How do I delete my data?

Since the data is anonymized, we have no way to associate the data you provide. However, this analytic data is summarized and the original, specific data are deleted after a period of time.
